# Mechanism for ice growth on the surface of a spherical water droplet
This repository contains the data used for the figures within the project "Mechanism for ice growth on the surface of a spherical water droplet."
The final paper was published on Febuary 21st 2025 in Physical Review B. 

The data can be found within the file "data.xls"

## Links
[Link to published article](https://journals.aps.org/prb/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevB.111.075426)

[Link to prepint arXiv version](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.13155)



# Dataset: Mechanism for ice growth on the surface of a spherical water droplet
---

# 1. Dataset Description

This dataset contains the numerical simulation data, figure source files, and scripts used in the study of **Mechanism for ice growth on the surface of a spherical water droplet**.
